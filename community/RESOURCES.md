## External starting points

These are primary standards or broad discovery hubs, not endorsements. Verify the current document, license, terms, and fit before relying on any resource.

| Resource | Use it for | Verify before use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [NIST AI Risk Management Framework](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) | A risk-management vocabulary and operating frame | Which profile and current publication apply to your context |
| [OECD AI Principles](https://oecd.ai/en/ai-principles) | International policy principles and definitions | Applicable local law and current guidance |
| [OWASP GenAI Security Project](https://genai.owasp.org/) | Security risks, guidance, and community projects for generative AI | The current project/version and relevance to your architecture |
| [AI Incident Database](https://incidentdatabase.ai/) | Reported real-world AI harms and failure patterns | Source quality, reporting bias, and similarity to your use case |
| [GitHub Awesome](https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome) | A directory of community-curated technical lists | Each list's maintenance, inclusion policy, and license |
| [Papers with Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) | Research papers linked to implementation and benchmark material | Reproducibility, benchmark fit, and repository status |

Last reviewed: **2026-07-12**. A review date means a human checked the link and description; it does not make fast-changing facts permanent.

## What belongs

A proposed resource should:

- solve a clear job in an AoT workflow;
- have a stable public reference;
- add something not already covered by the repository's software directory;
- be describable without unverifiable superlatives;
- include a limitation or question to verify;
- disclose the submitter's affiliation and material AI assistance.

Collections are judged by maintenance and inclusion policy, not star count. Individual commercial tools belong in the [software submission path](../software/SUBMIT.md), not here.

## Submission record

Use the Resource Exchange discussion form. Include:

```text
Name and canonical URL:
Resource type:
Job it supports:
Evidence or first-hand use:
What to verify:
Maintainer / provenance:
Last checked:
Affiliation:
AI assistance:
```

A steward can accept a resource into this page through a PR, redirect it to the software directory, request evidence, or decline it with a rationale. Links are periodically rechecked; stale or unsafe entries are corrected or removed without preserving them for list size.
